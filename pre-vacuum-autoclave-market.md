<p><p>The pre-vacuum autoclave market comprises two main types: horizontal and vertical autoclaves. Horizontal autoclaves feature a sideways opening door and are typically used for larger volumes and bulk sterilization, making them ideal for hospitals and laboratories. In contrast, vertical autoclaves have a top-opening design, suitable for smaller spaces and lower volume needs, often used in dental and research settings. Both types effectively create a vacuum to remove air, ensuring thorough sterilization of instruments and materials.</p></p>
