<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive touch switch market is primarily categorized into Type I and Type II segments. Type I encompasses capacitive touch switches, which rely on the touch sensor's ability to detect finger presence through capacitance changes, enabling sleek and modern designs. Type II includes resistive touch switches, which function by pressure applied to the screen, allowing for use in varied conditions, such as when wearing gloves. Both types enhance user interfaces in vehicles, promoting safety and convenience while driving.</p></p>
