<p><p>The Disposable Hemoperfusion Device market includes two primary types: charcoal pellet diffusers and resin pellet infusers. Charcoal pellet diffusers utilize activated charcoal to adsorb toxins and other harmful substances from the bloodstream, making them effective for various poisoning cases. Resin pellet infusers, on the other hand, utilize resin beads to selectively bind specific toxins or metabolites, offering targeted purification. Both devices are essential in therapeutic applications, improving patient outcomes in conditions associated with toxic overload or organ failure.</p></p>
