<p><p>The Driver Night Vision Goggles market features two primary types: LLL (Light Leveling Low-light) Night Vision Goggles and Infrared Night Vision Goggles. LLL Night Vision Goggles amplify available ambient light, allowing for visibility in low-light conditions, making them ideal for nighttime driving. In contrast, Infrared Night Vision Goggles detect thermal radiation emitted by objects, enabling users to see heat sources in complete darkness. Both technologies enhance visibility and safety for drivers navigating challenging nighttime environments.</p></p>
