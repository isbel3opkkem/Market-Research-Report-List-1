<p><p>The Bluetooth Audio Chips and Modules Market comprises two main types: Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chips and Audio System-on-Chip (SoC). Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chips focus on basic audio transmission functions, providing essential connectivity for devices like headphones and speakers. In contrast, Audio SoC integrates advanced processing capabilities, combining audio processing, Bluetooth connectivity, and often additional features like noise cancellation and voice assistance in a single chip. This enhances performance, reduces component count, and improves overall audio quality, catering to more sophisticated audio applications.</p></p>
