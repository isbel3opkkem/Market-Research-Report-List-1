<p><p>The Vehicle Emission Testers market features various types, each serving specific testing needs. NDIR testers measure gas concentrations using infrared light, ensuring accuracy in detecting emissions. Flame ionization detectors are used for hydrocarbons, providing high sensitivity. Smoke meters assess particulate emissions from diesel engines. Cross duct testers analyze emissions from both directions in exhaust systems. Emission and combustion testers with chillers evaluate engine performance and emissions under varied cooling conditions. Other testers encompass a range of innovative technologies for comprehensive vehicle emission analysis.</p></p>
