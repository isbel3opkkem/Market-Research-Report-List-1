<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Silicon IR (Infrared) Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Short-Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon IR camera market is segmented into four main types based on wavelength ranges. Near Infrared (NIR) cameras detect short wavelengths from 0.75 to 1.4 microns and are often used in surveillance and agriculture. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) cameras, spanning 1.4 to 3 microns, excel in material analysis and industrial applications. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) cameras, covering 3 to 8 microns, are utilized in military and thermal imaging. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) cameras, ranging from 8 to 14 microns, are primarily used for thermal detection and monitoring.</p></p>
