<p><p>The chemical drums market primarily consists of three types: steel drums, plastic drums, and fiber drums. Steel drums are durable and ideal for transporting hazardous materials, offering excellent protection against physical damage and corrosion. Plastic drums are lightweight, resistant to chemicals, and suitable for a wide range of applications, making them popular for non-hazardous liquids. Fiber drums, often made from cardboard, are eco-friendly and suitable for dry and semi-solid materials, providing an economical option for various industrial uses.</p></p>
