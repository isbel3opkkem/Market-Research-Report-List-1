<p><strong>The Total Knee Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Knee Systems</li><li>Partial Knee Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Total Knee Replacement market includes two main types: Primary Knee Systems and Partial Knee Systems. Primary Knee Systems are designed for total joint replacement, addressing severe arthritis or knee damage to restore function and relieve pain. They typically involve replacing the entire knee joint with a prosthetic implant. In contrast, Partial Knee Systems target only the damaged compartment of the knee, preserving healthy bone and tissue. This option is less invasive and can lead to quicker recovery and improved mobility for suitable patients.</p></p>
