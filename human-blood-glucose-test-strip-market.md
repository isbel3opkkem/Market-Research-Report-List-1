<p><p>The human blood glucose test strip market comprises various types based on different enzymatic methods. Glucose oxidase test strips utilize glucose oxidase enzymes to oxidize glucose, producing hydrogen peroxide. PQQ-glucose dehydrogenase strips employ pyrroloquinoline quinone for enhanced sensitivity. FAD-glucose dehydrogenase strips use flavin adenine dinucleotide for accurate glucose measurement, while hexokinase-based strips measure glucose levels through a phosphorylation reaction. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of accuracy, speed, and operational requirements, catering to diverse user needs in diabetes management.</p></p>
