<p><strong>The Metal Cutting Fluid and Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Cutting Fluids</li><li>Cutting Oils</li><li>Emulsions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal cutting fluid and oil market includes three primary types: water-based cutting fluids, cutting oils, and emulsions. Water-based cutting fluids are primarily composed of water and additives, offering cooling and lubrication while being less toxic and easier to clean. Cutting oils, derived from petroleum, provide superior lubrication and thermal stability but can be more challenging to handle. Emulsions blend oil and water, combining the benefits of both, offering effective cooling and lubrication while being more versatile for various machining processes.</p></p>
