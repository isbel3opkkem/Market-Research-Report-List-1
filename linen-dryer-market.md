<p><ul><li>Fluidized Bed Dryers</li><li>Conduction Dryers</li><li>Convection Dryers</li><li>Rotary Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linen dryer market encompasses various drying technologies, each suited for specific needs. Fluidized bed dryers utilize hot air to suspend and dry linen particles efficiently. Conduction dryers rely on direct contact with heated surfaces to transfer heat, suitable for high moisture content. Convection dryers circulate hot air around linens, promoting even drying and flexibility in capacity. Rotary dryers leverage a rotating drum mechanism for homogenous drying, ideal for continuous processing. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the linen’s moisture level and processing time.</p></p>
