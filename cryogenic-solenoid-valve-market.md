<p><p>The cryogenic solenoid valve market consists of three main types: Plunger Valves, Direct-Acting Plunger Valves, and Pilot-Operated Valves. Plunger Valves use a simple mechanism to control flow by moving a plunger in and out. Direct-Acting Plunger Valves operate without the need for a pressure differential, providing immediate response for low-pressure applications. Pilot-Operated Valves utilize a pilot valve to manage higher pressure systems, enhancing efficiency and control in cryogenic applications. All types are essential for regulating cryogenic fluids safely.</p></p>
